Application Requirements

For Data 2 (about dialysis centers in Comunidad Valenciana) the application idea could be to develop a software tool in order to analyze the overloading of the dyalisis centers based on their location, the company managing them, etc. This application could be used by public health administrations in order to manage the needs of the different health centers.

For Data Hoteles (about the hoteles in valencia) the application could suggest you which hotel is your most desired one in terms address, price and rating
